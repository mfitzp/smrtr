QWhat is SQ?

SQ or "smrtr Quotient" is the measure of your relative intelligence on the smrtr system. Just like an SQ it is calculated from your performance on the questions your attempt, and relative to other people.

As with an IQ, the average SQ is always 100, and you can score anywhere between 0 and 200. As other people's SQ scores go up, yours will go down. So you need to keep working to stay on top!
